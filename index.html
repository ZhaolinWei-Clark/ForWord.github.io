Company Profile | 公司简介

Anyang FORWORD Machinery Limited Company specializes in the production of electric spindles and mechanical spindles. We boast reliable design and manufacturing technologies for electric and machine tool spindles, advanced equipment, and dependable quality.

The company currently employs dozens of technical engineers and professional sales personnel, all with decades of industry experience. We place great emphasis on technological innovation and new product development, researching and developing various new products that suit the trends of both international and domestic markets.

Our products are primarily used in CNC machining centers, high-speed internal and external grinding, CNC lathes, flat grinding, high-speed milling, woodworking machinery, and more. Upholding the principles of "Sincerity, Pragmatism, Innovation, and Excellence," our business philosophy centers on "Reliable Quality, User-Centric, Technically Sound, and Comprehensive Service." We are capable of developing and designing various spindles according to special user requirements.

公司简介 | Company Profile

安阳市福沃德机械有限责任公司是生产电主轴和机械主轴的专业厂家，拥有可靠的电主轴、机床主轴设计和制造技术、设备精良、品质可靠。

公司现有技术工程师和专业销售人员数十人，均从事该行业几十年。公司十分注重科技创新和新产品研发，根据国际国内市场的发展趋势，研发适用的各类新产品。

产品主要适用于数控加工中心、高速内外圆磨削、数控车床、平面磨削、高速雕铣、木工机械等。公司恪守“诚心、务实、创新、求精”的理念，以“质量可靠、用户需求为主、技术可靠、服务完备”为经营宗旨，能够根据用户的特殊要求开发设计各种主轴。
